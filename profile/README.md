Information-driven Product Development & Engineering (IdPDE)

Welcome to the GitHub organization of the Information-driven Product Development & Engineering (IdPDE) research group at the University of Twente.

Our research develops methods, tools, and software to support decision-making throughout the product and production system lifecycle. By taking an information-driven perspective, we investigate how digital technologies, engineering methods, and data can be used to improve the design, operation, and evolution of products and manufacturing systems.

This organisation serves as the central home for our research projects, software, documentation, educational materials, and reusable tools. Each repository documents a specific project.

For more information about the research group, visit the [IdPDE website](https://www.utwente.nl/en/et/dpm/chair/IdPDE/).
