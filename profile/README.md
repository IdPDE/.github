Information-driven Product Development & Engineering (IdPDE)

Welcome to the GitHub organization of the Information-driven Product Development & Engineering (IdPDE) research group at the University of Twente.

Our research develops methods, tools, and software to support decision-making throughout the product and production system lifecycle. By taking an information-driven perspective, we investigate how digital technologies, engineering methods, and data can be used to improve the design, operation, and evolution of products and manufacturing systems. For more information about the research group, visit the [IdPDE website](https://www.utwente.nl/en/et/dpm/chair/IdPDE/).

This GitHub organisation serves as the central home for the research projects, software, documentation, educational materials, and reusable tools of IdPDE. Each repository documents a specific project.
More information on how to use this organisation can be found [here](https://github.com/IdPDE/.github/tree/main).
